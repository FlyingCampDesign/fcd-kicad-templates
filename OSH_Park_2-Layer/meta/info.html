OSH Park 2-Layer Prototype Service

This project template sets up the manufacturing design rules for the OSH Park 2-Layer Prototype Service according to the instructions at https://docs.oshpark.com/design-tools/kicad/kicad-design-rules/.

Fabrication Specs

  • 2 layers, parts on front and back

  • 63 mil (1.6mm) board thickness

  • 2 mil solder mask clearance (https://docs.oshpark.com/tips+tricks/stop-mask-expansion/)

  • 4 mil (0.1016mm) solder mask minimum width (web)

  • 6 mil (0.1524mm) minimum track width

  • 6 mil (0.1524mm) minimum clearance

  • 10 mil (0.254mm) minimum drill

  • 20 mil (0.508mm) minimum via dia (10 mil drill + 5 mil annular ring)

  • 5 mil (0.127mm) minimum hole to hole

  • No blind or buried vias allowed

  • No micro vias allowed
